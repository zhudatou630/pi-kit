# Review Checklist

Run this before delivering the final `.docx`. The banned-phrase lists live in `references/style-guide.md`; this checklist verifies they were applied rather than re-listing them.

## Source And Fact Checks

- Company name, stock name, date, and meeting type match the folder/user request.
- Main transcript was the core source for meeting-specific statements.
- Handwritten notes were used for emphasis and possible corrections, not as the sole source for hard numbers.
- Proper nouns, product names, customer names, and technical terms checked against local references or public sources when uncertain.
- Suspicious numbers verified, hedged, or removed.
- The memo does not silently mix contradictory source claims.

## Opening And Structure

- Opening label matches meeting type: 调研→调研要点; 交流/反路演/电话会→交流要点; short note→总结.
- Summary bullets lead with facts, not thesis frames; no slogan openers; no trailing directives.
- Section labels are topic-only; no slogan subtitles; dramatic words softened.
- Body sections organized by business line, not transcript order.
- A 后续跟踪 section, if present, is one line per item with only verifiable tracking variables and no risk items; or it is omitted.

## Memo Quality

- Repeated answers and low-value Q&A collapsed.
- Important drivers covered where relevant: orders, capacity, customers, product value, margins, delivery/recognition, catalysts, risks.
- Memo is meaningfully shorter than the transcript.
- Tone is professional, buy-side, in the analyst's own internal voice.

## Anti-AI / Anti-Transcript Pass (verify against style-guide banned lists)

- No third-party buy-side framing (对买方跟踪而言 / 供买方参考 / ...).
- No self-referential document wording (正文 / 本纪要 / ...).
- No meeting-process narration (本次交流口径 / 会中口径 / 调研结束后交流 / 公司方面表示 / ...).
- No embedded valuation/method/meaning commentary sentences (短期估值弹性来自 / 盈利端不宜过度外推 / 现阶段估值测算不应简单按 / ...).
- No AI-flavored transitions (综上所述 / 不是……而是…… / 有望打开广阔空间 / ...).
- No generic thesis labels without concrete evidence (逻辑切换 / 估值修复 / 核心逻辑 / 打开第二增长曲线 / ...).
- No transcript artifacts ("嗯", "然后呢", "这个的话"); no malformed bullets; no Q&A that repeats the body.
- Obvious background that does not change the judgment has been removed.

## Word Output

- The `.docx` exists and opens.
- The `.md` draft is kept alongside the `.docx`.
- No large title unless requested.
- Section labels render as bold body text, not Word title/heading styles.
- Section labels do not end with `。` or `.`.
- Blank lines between major sections and before important sublabels.
- If uncertainties remain, a separate `待确认事项.md` exists and the final memo does not overstate those points.
